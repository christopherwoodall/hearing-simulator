Auditory Simulation

This application synthesizes specific hearing phenomena using the native Web Audio API.

Phenomena Simulator

Wave-like Tinnitus

A 3000Hz sine wave tone that smoothly fades in and out every 2 seconds. Starts at a very low 1% volume.

Constant 6000Hz Tone

A pure, continuous sine wave fixed at 6000Hz. This represents a classic high-frequency sensorineural tinnitus tone. Starts at 3%.

Muffled Talking (Paper Bag)

Simulates hearing speech through a barrier. Generates noise, applies a strict 400Hz lowpass filter, and modulates amplitude to mimic syllables.

Tensor Tympani Rumble

Simulates the sound of voluntarily flexing the muscle inside the middle ear (ear popping). Creates a brief 50Hz low-frequency thrum.
