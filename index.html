Auditory Simulation

This application synthesizes specific hearing phenomena using the native Web Audio API. Warning: Contains high-pitched and potentially grating frequencies.

Phenomena Simulator

Ringing Phone Tinnitus

Simulates a phantom telephone ring. Uses two beating oscillators (440Hz & 480Hz) routed through a fast LFO for the mechanical "trill", and programmed to pulse on a 2-second on / 4-second off cadence.

Constant 6000Hz Tone

A pure, continuous sine wave fixed at 6000Hz. This represents a classic high-frequency sensorineural tinnitus tone. Keep volume low to avoid ear strain.

Muffled Talking (Paper Bag)

Simulates hearing speech through a barrier. Generates white noise, applies a strict 400Hz lowpass filter, and modulates the amplitude randomly to mimic the syllables and cadence of human conversation.
